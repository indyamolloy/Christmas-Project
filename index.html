Three Card Spread

This spread gives you an overview of the past, present and future. It can serve as an invaluable fast-focus before an important event. You can also highlight one key area of your life, such as finances or love and select cards that relate purely to these concerns.

  • 1: The past
  • 2: The present
  • 3: The future

Seven Card Progression

The seven card spread is designed to give you deeper insight into past and future influences and events. The pattern of the spread consists of two three-card sequences with a signifcator,a central single card which expresses the nature of whaat is in your mind, such as current opportunities, worries or people.

  • 1: The past
  • 2: How you saw yourself at this time
  • 3: What you learned
  • 4: Your present circumstances
  • 5: New influences about to enter your life
  • 6: How they will affect you
  • 7: The outcome

The Question-And-Answer Spread

If you have a specific decision to make, or an unresolved issue, the seven cards in this spread reveal the hidden influences in your life that affect the question. The layout symbolically breaks down a problem into manageable aspects.

  • 1: The past
  • 2: The present: the question in hand
  • 3: Hidden influences that need to be considered
  • 4: The attitudes of others around you
  • 5: The best action to take
  • 6: Obstacles in your path
  • 7: The final outcome

The Celtic Cross

The celtic cross is a traditional Tarot spread used to present a general life overview, or to explore and predict the outcome to a specific question. The central cross shape formed by cards 1 and 2 can be interpreted as what complements or crosses you at present.

  • 1: Your current circumstances
  • 2: What is helping or hindering you
  • 3: The best you can do at present
  • 4: Hidden factors around you
  • 5: The effect of the past
  • 6: Your next move
  • 7: How you see yourself
  • 8: How others see you
  • 9: Hopes or fears
  • 10: The outcome

The Week Ahead Spread

This spread is an ideal way to gain insight into an important week ahead. Each card relates to a day of the week. The significator, or key, card is laid first, and foretells the flavor of the whole week.

  • 1: Monday
  • 2: Tuesday
  • 3: Wednesday
  • 4: Thursday
  • 5: Friday
  • 6: Saturday
  • 7: Sunday
